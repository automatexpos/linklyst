Privacy Policy

Last updated: October 9, 2025

Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly to us, such as when you create an account, update your profile, or contact us for support.

Personal Information:

  • Email address
  • Username
  • Profile information (display name, bio, profile picture)
  • Links and content you add to your profile

Analytics Information:

  • Link clicks and usage statistics
  • Basic device and browser information
  • IP addresses (for security purposes)

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain our service
  • Create and display your public profile
  • Provide analytics about link performance
  • Communicate with you about your account
  • Improve our services

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share information in the following circumstances:

  • With your consent
  • To comply with legal obligations
  • To protect our rights and safety

Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal information
  • Update or correct your information
  • Delete your account and associated data
  • Export your data

Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us through our support form in your dashboard.
